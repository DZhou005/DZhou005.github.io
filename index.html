About Me

My name is Danny Zhou. I graduated from University of California, Riverside with a degree in business administration concentrating in management. While I was in college I took an intro to computer science course and that is where I initially realized my love for software development. I finally decided to pursue my passion and applied to App Academy. That is where I became proficient at coding and that is also where I sharpend my skills as a software engineer. Outside of coding, some of my interests/hobbies are video games such as Leauge of Legends and Valorant and badminton.

Projects

Clip It

Clip It is a user-friendly website where people can come together and upload their amazing gaming highlights via Amazon Web Services and share it with everyone on the website to see! While viewing the video, users can do various actions such as liking or commenting while on the video page. This was all done with Python in the backend and JavaScript in the frontend.

Technologies Used: React, Redux, Python, JavaScript, Flask, SQLAlchemy, CSS, AWS S3, Postgres
